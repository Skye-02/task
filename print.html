How to Connect a Printer using Bluetooth

How to Connect a Printer using Bluetooth. Bluetooth is a most powerful wireless technology that lets your computer find and share devices. Connecting a printer using
Bluetooth is very simple.

  • Step 1: First you make sure your printer Bluetooth is enabled or not. Some printers are connected manually, but others require adapters.
  • Step 2: Now Set up your adapter. All you need only do is connect one end to your computer and the other end to your printer, and it will be installed itself.
  • Step 3: Turn on the printer. First make sure it is connected to working electrical outlet or not and the printer power is on correctly.
  • Step 4: Open Bluetooth devices on your PC. After that installing your Bluetooth adapter and go in to the control panel and open "Bluetooth Devices."
  • Step 5: Add printers automatically. This is easier, but less secure. From the options tab, you have to make sure the options "Turn on to find" and "Allow Bluetooth devices to connect to this computer" are checked. Then Your printer and computer then find each other automatically and begin working. If you'd rather not let other devices find in your computer, you follow the steps 6 and 7 instead.
  • Step 6: Add devices manually. From the devices tab, select the device and "Add Device" and run the installation wizard to find your printer.
  • Step 7: Use the "Add Printers" wizard. You can also add the printer by clicking on the "Start" and "Printers and Faxes" and then "Add a Printer." When the wizard pop up,select "Bluetooth printer" and Windows will search for your printer automatically.

How to set up a new printer and scanner


  • Step 1:Plug in the printer's and scanner power cable and make sure it's turned on.

  • Step 2: Connect the USB cable from the printer to the computer. Note: The process will be
    different if you are using a wireless printer and scanner; we will talk more about that later on.

  • Step 3: On your computer, locate the Printer settings. If you are using any version of Windows
    computer, you will find these in the Control Panel. If you are using a Mac Computer, you will
    find them in System Preferences.

  • Step 4: Look for the option to Add a printer and scanner, then follow the instructions that
    appear.

  • Step 5:Now it's time to try printing something! For example, you could try printing and
    scanning this test page. After you click the link, select File > Print from your web browser's
    menu, or simply press Ctrl+P button on your keyboard (or Command+P if you're on a Mac).

  • Step 6:A dialog box will appear. Locate and select the new printer and scanner from the list,
    then click Print.

  • Step 7:The item will then be printed. But if it doesn't work, don't panic! You can try using
    some of the other solutions to common problems Click on Read More.
